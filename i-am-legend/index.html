I Am Legend

2015-03-28

It offers among the most impressive sets in movie history: a Manhattan so deserted that lions and impalas walk its streets. The city is also filled with rusting vehicles, including military tanks and planes. Too kool.

Emma Thompson, though appearing briefly, does offer the movie's most electrifying performance. Will Smith, on the other hand, shows a great lack of acting skills, and is even embarrassing at times. He is, however, interesting enough to lead the story well.

The movie was a great first-time experience, but the revisits were lesser by far.
