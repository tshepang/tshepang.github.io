Austin Powers: International Man of Mystery (1997)

2012-11-16

There is good humor here and there, some of which is unique. Check for example the opening scene, and every musical and photo sequence. Trouble is that some of the jokes just miss the spot, while the hilarity of some totally disappear on a revisit.

One low point of the movie is the miscast Elizabeth Hurley, whose persona (or acting ability) simply does not fit.
