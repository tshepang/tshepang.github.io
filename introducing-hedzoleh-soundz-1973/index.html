Introducing Hedzoleh Soundz (1973)

2013-11-16

Several months ago, I bumped onto this jazz album by Hugh Masekela. It sounds quite different to his more popular tracks, and although it has a South African feel to it (Masekela is a citizen of that country), the more dominating feel is from elsewhere in Africa (just don't ask me where).

My favorites:

  • track 3: Adade
  • track 4: Yei Baa Gbe Wolo
  • track 6: When
  • track 7: Nye Tamo Ame
  • track 8: Rekpete
