How To Train Your Dragon (2010)

2014-07-21

It was lots more impressive on first viewing. This time around, I found the face animation terrible, the story recycled, complete with tired humor, and a forced villain (why couldn't it be trained as well, and what's the story... why is it so big). Luckily the whole thing is not boring, and there are lots of scary moments and edge-of-seat action. The concept of befriending dragons is also interesting.
