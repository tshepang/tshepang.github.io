Arthur (1981)

2012-06-02

The world's critics amaze me again, with their love for this uninteresting and (mostly) unfunny comedy. Someone even nominated it for its script. WTF! The sequel is a lot better.

There is some appeal to the lead performer, but he's mostly just annoying. None of the other performances are particularly good, except of course the guy playing the caregiver, who never fucks up anyways.
