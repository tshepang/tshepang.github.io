Grown Ups (2010)

2011-08-01

There isn't much going for this one, except I admire one of the characters (in his early forties) falling in love with an elderly lady. The movie is not unbearable but it should be given a miss. Chris Rock shows again his extreme lack of acting skill and Salma Hayek hasn't lost her position as among the most beautiful women on the planet. That old lady gives a nice performance BTW and she looks pretty for her age.
