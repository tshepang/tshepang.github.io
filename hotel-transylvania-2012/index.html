Hotel Transylvania (2012)

2013-03-09

Nothing fresh here as I expected, judging by the trailer. There is also that annoying and all-too-common thing of someone lying and later regretting and apologising in public. Movie is shallow too, and each character recycled.

I did like some of the dance sequences though (regardless of the badness of the music), and I thought the dirty chef character was very well-animated, and is a showcase of quality character design.
