Upside Down (2012)

2014-01-18

It got a great concept (the most original I've seen in a while) and great visuals. But after the novelty wears off about half-way through, there's pretty much nothing left. I enjoyed the performance of Timothy Spall, but his character was not well-developed. None of the other characters are given quality treatment either.
