The Headless Woman (2008)

2011-04-25

I had to watch this one a few times in pieces because it was too boring to bear. It's a sleep-inducing exercise, following an extremely uninteresting semi-retarded lady who is suffering from an untold incident, as well as the guilt of (potentially) hitting a human with her car. We are led to keep hoping that something will actually happen, but nothing does. Luckily the acting is good and so is direction and camerawork.

[sidenote] There is one critic who found it great, inspiring me to give it a second look. It's a better and deeper review than mine.
