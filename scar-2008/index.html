Scar (2008)

2011-04-25

A run-of-the-mill horror that, luckily, has pretty gals and decent camerawork. There is nothing new and the twist is underwhelming. What I like about it is that it's not pretentious, and is devoid of arrogance.

When people spend so much effort on a movie, I wonder how they the justify the question, what makes this stand above the rest?.
