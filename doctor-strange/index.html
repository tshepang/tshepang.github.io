Doctor Strange

2016-11-22

Am not a fan of highly complicated action sequences, though luckily they weren't too long or loud in here. I also enjoyed the title character before being transformed into a sorcerer, reminding me of the excellent Dr House (the tv series), and also given a good performance. I did, however, find his transformation into a great sorcerer rushed. The bargaining scene was interesting, though I wish the set was a lot less cartoonish. There were some other stupid things, like the sorcerers running around chasing each other, when they had the powers to control objects by mere willpower, one of those "why the fuck didn't you do that in the first place" moments. I wish the black shit on the faces of the evil sorcerers should have been explained as well... it looks so stupid, like kids playing with mud.
