2024-07-13

Her boasts best editing, best cinematography, and most exciting story concept. Elysium has the best visual design: see the vehicles (especially the gorgeous one carrying the billionaire), the robot police, and the ring world.

The best performance is that of Geoffrey Rush in The Best Offer (such impressive voice acting and character development), while the most desirable character is that of Sarah Gadon in Enemy, followed by that of Rachelle Lefevre in White House Down,

The most surprising movie is No Man's Land, because it is the only China movie I watched that is either a dark comedy or a Western.
