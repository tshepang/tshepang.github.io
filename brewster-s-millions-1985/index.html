Brewster's Millions (1985)

2011-12-30

A very delightful and shallow comedy about a guy who is to spend millions within a month for an even bigger reward. Richard Pryor, the lead, is fun to watch and so is the story, especially early on. The best act in the movie is the lead's political campaign. I can vaguely remember it, so I consider among the funniest movies I've watched this year.

[sidenote] Richard Pryor style of comedy makes Chris Rock look like a sorry imitator.
