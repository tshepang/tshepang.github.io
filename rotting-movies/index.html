rotting movies

2007-06-07

I recently revisited The Fifth Element (which I honored for its eye-candy) and The Boondock Saints (which I honored for its stylistic action and Willem Dafoe) and it's a pity that the experience wasn't so pleasant. Both actually emerged as bad movies for their shallow storylines with all appeal gone, except that The Boondock Saints' Willem Dafoe remaines interesting.
