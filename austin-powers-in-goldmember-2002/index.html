Austin Powers in Goldmember (2002)

2012-11-17

The least interesting of the three movies in the series, complete with a badly miscast Beyonce Knowles, who is even worse than Elizabeth Hurley in the first one. This is made even worse by her failed tough 70's black gal persona, a bad parody of Foxy Brown. The most annoying, and worst-developed, character however is the guy named Goldmember. What were they thinking? Luckily, Fat Bustard is interesting this time around, and so is the son of Dr. Evil.
