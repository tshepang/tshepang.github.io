The Man from Earth

For a movie that is almost entirely made of dialog, this is exciting. It's also the first time I saw the Tony Todd play an ordinary character, which he does very well.

I wish there would be more restraint: the overreaction by some of the characters was ludicrous and felt forced, and the Jesus part even worse.
