favorite laptop

2008-10-18

The laptop that I previously mentioned is named Compaq/HP nx7010 and is the most favorite I've ever used (despite that it's only the second laptop I've ever used heavily). I suspect I like it mostly because of the feel of the keyboard, and its 1.7GHz Pentium M is really more than adequate for the modest tasks I throw at it; and its only set-back is the placement of the 3 USB ports - at the back. It's a pity that I'll have to leave it behind since I'm changing jobs. I also use an external USB mouse, which also enhances the experience. [update]
