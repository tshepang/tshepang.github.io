Good People (2014)

2014-11-15

It has good direction and acting (I've never seen Tom Wilkinson this good, and I enjoyed watching the ultra-cool Omar Sy). But, it's yet another example of a well-made movie that ends not so well. It's too smart and fresh for such improbable things to happen, worst of which was having the detective drive into a bad guy just-in-time. Regardless, the critics gave it more hate than it deserved.
