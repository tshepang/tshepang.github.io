Tron: Legacy (2010)

2011-03-05

Bizarre sci-fi taking place mostly in a computer system, about an exciting wish for one computer program to escape into the real world. There's interesting artefacts, like a small rod that transforms into a vehicle at will, while the simulation of Jeff Bridge's face is disappointing, especiallly considering the great work done to Brad Pitt's face in The Curious Case of Benjamin Button, which was released in 2008. The imagery is very (too) stylish. None of the performances are worthy of praise (did they have to cast Olivia Wilde!?).
