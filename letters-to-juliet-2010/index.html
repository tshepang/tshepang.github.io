Letters To Juliet (2010)

2011-06-26

Exquisite cinematography, (excessively) loving characters, and some of the best depictions of Italian food and romance. The lead character looks so damn nice at the wedding. The movie is overlong (it drags) and has some unbearable romance. I like romance, but not when it's so blatantly formulaic. Maybe if I was a stereotypical female, I'd melt.
