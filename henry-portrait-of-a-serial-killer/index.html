Henry: Portrait of a Serial Killer

2013-05-26

A very enjoyable story with some very interesting performances, most notably that of Tom Towles, who plays a rather disgusting character, Otis. AFAIR, this is the only one of his performances I ever enjoyed, and he's just perfect. Michael Rooker is also a joy to watch, and despite his incredible cruelty, remains a guy we all care for, perhaps due to his care for the (delightful) leading lady.
