Love Actually (2003)

2011-12-27

There's a delightful/fresh feel to it and there's a bunch of fun moments, but the whole thing is spoilt when it goes into typical romantic comedy mode near the end, things like dozens of people following a man who intends to propose, or a kid breaching airport security to go mention his love for some gal. Bill Nighy and his manager were fun to watch.
