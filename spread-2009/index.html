Spread (2009)

2012-11-10

Quality direction, production design, cinematography, and cast. A decent story which luckily doesn't end in the tired way romances tend to be. I enjoyed this more the first time around, mainly due to the sexuality surrounding the man-whore and the rich lawyer. I enjoyed the open-mindedness that she expressed, though she later on became attached/possessive enough to find the jealousy unbearable.
