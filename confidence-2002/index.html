Confidence (2002)

1 minute read 2012-07-22

There's too much of an attempt at doing kool, much like Lucky Number Slevin (which was made some years later), only with a lower budget and less flash (production design, cinematography). It's however a lot more satisfying. I loved John Carrol Lynch's performance. Morris Chestnut, a not-so-competent actor, is miscast, and so is Dustin Hoffman, who appears to be trying too hard.
