Basic Instinct (1992)

2011-08-01

Though not as enjoyable as I was hoping this revisit to be, Sharon Stone is still quite a pleasure to watch. Michael Douglas isn't very interesting nor is anyone else. The sexual energy was rather silly (did we need to have near-rape in the story). Also, the movie isn't aging well (check the 80's fashion for one).
