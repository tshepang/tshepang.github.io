revisiting 'Unleashed'

2007-06-11

Bob Hoskins is not convincing in his villain role nor is Jet Li who is even worse, playing a lowly man-child; Morgan is miscast. Fight choreography is decent but the only really outstanding thing is the charming Kerry Condon playing the ever-smiling daughter. I last saw this one last year when released and it feels worse the second time around.
