2023 review

2024-10-28

The best movie is the mesmerizing El Conde. It also has the best script (great dialog that is infused with unusual humor, and a clever mix of the horror genre with real horrors inflicted by real monsters), and is probably the best vampire movie ever made.

My favorite character is Nimona in Nimona... fun, powerful, desirable.

The best comedy performance is by Huang Bo in Creation of the Gods I, but the best performance is by Paula Luchsinger in El Conde.

The best dialogue is in Anatomy of a Fall. The quarrel of the married couple is particularly notable.

The best combat is the final one in Boy Meets World... so intense, so brutal.

The best twist is in Strange Darling... so surprising!
