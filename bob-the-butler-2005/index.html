Bob The Butler (2005)

2012-07-13

It's a recycled story of a guy who appears to be a loser, but happens to be quite useful when put in some specific situation. I appreciate it because, regardless of being formulaic and shallow, it feels fresh -- it has unusual, laid-back direction, and quality comic performances from the lead and, most especially, the master butler. It screws up with it's needless use of slapstick.
