Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (2007)

2012-09-16

The battle between arch-villain and Dumbledore is the best action piece so far in this series. It's quite intense and has wonderful visual effects. There are other good visual effect moments here and there. Even the Dementors, which I so admired in the 3rd movie, have been given a facelift, though I prefer the original look.
