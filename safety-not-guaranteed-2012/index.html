Safety Not Guaranteed (2012)

Decent humor and an interesting lead make this watchable. There is good anticipation of whether or not the guy under study is a lunatic. The old girlfriend is desirable (as is the lead), and the carefree boss gets sweet at times, even if he is cardboard character. Weird small movie. Loved the ending. Romance was done okay, even if it is too typical of romantic comedies.
