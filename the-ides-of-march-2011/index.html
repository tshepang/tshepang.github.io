The Ides of March (2011)

2012-07-09

I expected a far more hard-hitting story, considering this is a modern and high-end political drama. What I got was not interesting enough to deserve going straight to cinema. It feels unfinished. Any random episode of The West Wing is superior.

The gal is very wantable though, and the wife of the presidential candidate (played by George Clooney) gives a decent performance, but is denied screen appearance.
