Horrible Bosses (2011)

2012-05-22

There is no shortage of hilarious moments, though none is as great as the pregnancy one; Jamie Foxx does a nice bit performance; the character of Kevin Spacey is rather overdone.

The movie is fun, but it sadly has a sketchy, amateurish feel of The 40-Year-Old Virgin or Office Space, both of which were also fun. It just feels like a collection of antics, instead of one solid whole worthy of being called a movie.
