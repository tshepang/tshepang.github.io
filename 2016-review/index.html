2016 review

2025-09-06

The best visual effects can be found in Deadpool, a movie that also boasts best action direction and humor.

The best sound can be found in Arrival, one used to represent alien verbal communication.

The most beautiful scene, which is also the most enchanting scene, is one where the toddler in Moana first meets the talking ocean.

My favorite character is Black Panther (Captain America: Civil War), followed by that of Leslie Jones in Ghostbusters.

The best character design is that of the Hollows of Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children.

The best performance is by Simon Helberg in Florence Foster Jenkins.

My favorite performance is that of Annalise Basso, due to the fierce and intense persona in Captain Fantastic.
