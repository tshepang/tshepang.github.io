X-Men: Days of Future Past (2014)

My favorite character is the super-fast mutant, and my favorite scene is one where it stops security guards from killing his accomplices. The humor is good and his abilities impressive. The time travel part wasn't done bad and Wolverine is interesting. The Sentinels are fearsome and the power of Magneto ridiculous, much like he had in X-Men 3: The Last Stand.

I see this received better reviews than any of the movies in the franchise, but I think the X-Men 2 was better.
