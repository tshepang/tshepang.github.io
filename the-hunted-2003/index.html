The Hunted (2003)

2008-05-01

The Hunted does a great job on engaging me in a way reminiscent of Bourne Identity trilogy given its intensity and edge-of-the-seat excitement as well as hyper-capable lead characters, and these leads (Benicio Del Toro and Tommy Lee Jones) provide top-class acts. By the way, the fight sequences are beyond any I've seen, bar oriental martial arts stuff.
