unforgiving characters are annoying

2012-07-08

It is rather too common in passive movies where you would find a bunch of characters whose quality of life is improved by a protagonist, but when something goes wrong as a result of the work of that protagonist, they get to be unforgiving/ungrateful to a point of public scorn or physical violence. What makes this overly-common thing even more sad is that such moments tend to spoil some decent quality movies.

Here are some examples:

  • Cloudy With A Chance Of Meatballs
  • Robot
  • The Green Hornet
