The Amazing Spider-Man (2012)

It is not really a supreme reboot, but it is an okay one. It does have some exciting effects, and I most especially love the fights between the hero and the villain. The love interest is not interesting, nor is any of the characters who fill the movie. Well, maybe except the villain, but only when in lizard form. The story is merely okay, and is bad at times, e.g. the help the hero gets near the end with the cranes and all (WTF!).
