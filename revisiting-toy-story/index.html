revisiting 'Toy Story'

2007-05-19

I saw Toy Story for the second time today since several years ago and it hasn't really aged, except for the technology of course. I saw it after Monster's, Inc. which I considered the best in 3D-animation for a long time until now (story not so fresh; an irritating and formulaic one-eyed character). So Toy Story remains victorious and got the honor of being the first of its kind. It's even more enjoyable due to providing code-names for Debian releases. The evil character, Sid, is interesting to watch but my favorite is Rex for his innocent and ever-smiling character.
