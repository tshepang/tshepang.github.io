overly complex action sequences

1 minute read 2013-09-15

I am a fan of action sequences in movies, but only if the complexity is kept to some limit. Too often, A-class movies add a lot of complexity to the mix, as if that equates to excellence, or increases chances of selling more tickets. Here's examples from the most guilty:

  1. The Adventures Of Tintin: The Secret Of The Unicorn
  2. The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey
