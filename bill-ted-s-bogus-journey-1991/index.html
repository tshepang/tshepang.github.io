Bill & Ted's Bogus Journey (1991)

2011-02-07

I expected this sequel to be junk, but was pleasantly surprised to find it was about as exciting as the first, and perhaps funnier, and with richer content.

pros

  • The Grim Reaper was great fun to watch; one of the highlights was him challenging the duo, who were playing for their freedom from hell.
  • It was fun to watch the evil Bill & Ted.

cons

  • The novelty of freshness went away.
  • Keanu Reeves still sucks.
  • The genius scientist could have been more fun, and their melding seemed to serve no purpose.
  • I miss the splattering of comic one-liners that the original had.
