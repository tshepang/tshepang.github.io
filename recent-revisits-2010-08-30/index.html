I Think I Love My Wife & Seven Pounds are among those movies that have a not-so-common property of being about as good on a revisit as the first viewing.

There's hardly any other gal that has been given such a beloved portrayal as Will Smith's sweetheart in Seven Pounds.

I Think I Love My Wife is well-made and got some of the hottest collection of gals of all movies. It's a nice display of the challenge of resisting temptation.
