It (1990)

2011-03-27

Horrible horror without much going for it, except that the clown is quite interesting, given his humorous and sinister character (and laughter). Why they made the lady so sexually suggestive is strange. Oh, and the black guy, why do I smell Black activism? The (non)battle with the giant spider is ludicrous. It's one of those things that make it hard to believe that movie makers always watch their creations before release. They could have done better with that spider, even in 1990. Oh, and the whole thing is just left unexplained.
