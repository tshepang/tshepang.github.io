Thor (2011)

2012-11-04

Does not live up to the hype, some parts of the shallow story are embarrassing, and the humor is recycled. The visual effects are meh, and so is the costume design of the Asgards. Their city has a nice design though, excessive as it is.

The title character is gorgeous as fuck. I also like the concept of the mysterious gatekeeper, and the character. The silver robot is also interesting.

I enjoyed the movie more on a revisit.
