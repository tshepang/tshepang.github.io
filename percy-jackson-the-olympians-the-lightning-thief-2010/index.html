Percy Jackson & The Olympians: The Lightning Thief (2010)

2011-02-13

This is a weird one, with a silly title, and nice visual effects, and a desirable gal in the form of the title character's love interest. The script is really stupid (e.g. one woman withstands the abuses of a smelly man in order to keep her son safe, since the smell can hide the son's scent!) and the end empty like a mothafucka.
