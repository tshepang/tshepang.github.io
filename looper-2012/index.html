Looper (2012)

2012-12-25

I dislike the overly-loud banging sounds, which mostly emanate from the gunshots. This is from a very violent movie which cast Bruce Willis (he should retire), and Joseph Gordon-Levitt, whose face should have been left alone, especially since he does not resemble Bruce even a bit even after all the non-sense is put on his face, digital or not.

I am hoping this overrated non-sense will be the last time travel movie ever made, since the concept is utter crap (nonsensical). The story starts exciting enough but loses steam very soon after. None of the perfomers are solid, especially Jeff Daniels as villain.
